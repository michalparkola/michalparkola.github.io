The Three Levels of Asynchronous Collaboration

Level 1: Simulating the office online

At this level we're building the foundation that enables flexibility and helps improve work-life balance while maintaining or improving productivity. More importantly we're enabling higher-level practices to be built on top of the foundation.

At this level we're likely to need to:

  • Learn how to Work Together Anwyhere:
    • Set expectations for how to work together
    • Simulate office interactions online
    • Facilitate online meetings like a pro
    • Give and receive feedback
  • Communicate well in (small chunks of) writing
  • Follow through on agreements, keep each other accountable
    • keep progress transparent
    • communicate obstacles early: ask for help or renegotiate
    • get feedback and adjust quickly
