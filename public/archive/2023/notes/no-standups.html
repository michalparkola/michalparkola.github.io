The Three Levels of Asynchronous Collaboration

Level 2: NoStandups

At this level the flow of work is transparent enough such that everyone can see what's going on and make good decisions without the need for lengthy status and planning meetings. Real-time discussions are used for higher level sense-making, problem-solving and relationship-building.

At this level we're likely to need to:

  • align to needs at the start, in the middle and at the end
  • even better writing skills and habits
  • build a well-organized information system to capture things without getting flooded
  • strong alignment using clear, concise and meaningfull content & transparency of outcomes (metrics, dashboards, stories)
  • make the invisible, visible - "show me" is our mantra
  • have a living handbook of standard practices - separate law and advice
